Mass Transit in Philadelphia

The City of Philadelphia's transit needs are mainly served by the Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority. Within the city, the three major modes are heavy-rapid rail, light rail, and an extensive bus network. Although the system extends far outside Philadelphia's limits and Includes other modes such as Paratransit and commuter rail, these modes are not included in this data set.

The goal is this site is to introduce each of the modes and its coverage area in the city. NOTE, the bus lines will take a few seconds to load onto AND off of the map!
